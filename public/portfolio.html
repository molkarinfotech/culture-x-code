Ethnic Story — AI-Generated E-Commerce Platform

A full-scale Indian ethnic clothing store, designed and built entirely with AI-assisted development. From concept to deployment in record time.

Case Study · 2026

Ethnic Story is a modern Indian ethnic clothing e-commerce platform built to serve a new generation of fashion-forward shoppers who want culturally rich designs with a contemporary edge. The entire platform — from UI to backend — was architected and delivered using AI-powered development workflows.

The project demonstrates what's possible when deep technical expertise meets AI tooling: a production-ready, full-stack e-commerce site with advanced features delivered at startup speed.
